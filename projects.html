Over Fall Break, 13 students were split into groups and sent into Detroit to assist local non-profits with Data Analysis and UX projects. With a team of two other students, I had the chance to assist non-profit Arts & Scraps discover new ways to manage and visualize their data.

Within two days we were able to collaborate with the client and create a working deliverable that their volunteers can use to input and visualize feedback responses. This will help Arts & Scraps quantify their impact, which will in turn help them qualify for grants. Feel free to contact me for more information about the project specifics.
